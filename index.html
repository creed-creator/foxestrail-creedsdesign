Our Story

A Downtown Kokomo Institution

Tucked right in the heart of downtown Kokomo, The Foxes Trail has built a loyal following on one simple promise — great food, honest prices, and a room that feels like home.

From prime rib on the weekend to our famous grouper nuggets, every plate is made with care. Whether you're stopping in for a quick tenderloin sandwich or settling in for a steak dinner, we treat every guest like a regular.
